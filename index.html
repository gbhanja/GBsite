About Me

Research Profile

I am a Doctoral Researcher in the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar. My research focuses on Quantum Information Theory, Open Quantum Systems, Quantum Thermodynamics and Quantum Batteries.

My current work investigates non-Markovian quantum dynamics, dissipative systems and energy storage in quantum devices.
