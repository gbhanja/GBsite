About Me

Doctoral Researcher at the Department of Physics, Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar.

Research Profile

My research focuses on Quantum Information Theory, Open Quantum Systems and Quantum Thermodynamics. I am interested in understanding quantum correlations, dissipation, non-Markovian dynamics and quantum energy storage.

I enjoy combining analytical techniques with numerical simulations to investigate the fundamental limits of quantum technologies.
